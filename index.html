    About

  • I'm Jacob Stuart, a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Florida with a passion for Virtual Agents, Human-Computer Interaction, and AR/VR/XR. I've honed my skills in various tools and technologies, such as Unity3D, Lens Studio, Google Dialogflow, UI/UX tools, creative tools, and programming languages like C#, Python, and JavaScript.
  • During my time at UF, I've worked as a Graduate Research Assistant, collaborating with different institutions to create immersive augmented/virtual reality simulation-based trainings for healthcare students. I've also been a Graduate Teaching Assistant, supporting students and creating a positive learning environment.
  • As an Official Lens Creator in the Snap Lens Network, I've designed popular AR lenses for Snapchat that have been viewed more than 3 million times. I also participated in the Spectacles Creator Program, where I made medical simulation lenses and shared feedback on Snapchat's software and hardware.
  • I've co-authored several research publications, showcasing my expertise in virtual human interactions and how they affect user perceptions. My work has been published in respected journals and presented at international conferences, proving my ability to communicate complex research findings to a wide range of audiences.
  • As a dedicated researcher and educator, I'm excited to continue contributing to the fields of virtual agents and human-computer interaction, helping to shape the future of immersive technologies and their applications.
