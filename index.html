About Me

Bio

Jacob Stuart, PhD is a researcher and designer specializing in augmented and virtual reality (AR/VR), human-centered computing, and healthcare simulation. He earned his PhD in Human-Centered Computing from the University of Florida, where his work focused on leveraging immersive technologies to improve clinical education and training. Currently, he serves as a Research Projects Manager in the Healthcare Human Factors Lab at Emory University School of Medicine. His interdisciplinary work combines AR/VR development, simulation design, and human factors to create innovative training tools for healthcare professionals. Jacob’s research has been published in venues spanning computing, nursing, and infection prevention, and he remains passionate about building technology that enhances learning, empathy, and patient safety.
